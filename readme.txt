FYI, Please find the score calculation formula below:
====================
Scoring formula
====================
--------------------
Level Up
--------------------
+ 200 * L for passing level L
+ 1 * T for every second of remaining time T

--------------------
Kill Monsters
--------------------
+ 50 * M for every monster M killed

--------------------
Collect Good Things
--------------------
+ 10 * G for every good thing G collected
